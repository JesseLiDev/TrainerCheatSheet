About Trainerize Cheats

Trainerize Cheats is a web application designed to make researching your client's information much faster.
How it works:

  1. Enter in your trainerize login information on the login page.

 2. TrainerizeCheatSheet uses your credentials to directly talk to the Trainerize Server API to get all of your client's
nutrition and workout information in a matter of seconds. WE DO NOT SAVE YOUR LOGIN CREDENTIALS

 3. TrainerizeCheatSheet generates a report displaying the following information:

      A. An average of each of your client's Caloires, Protein, Carbs, and Fats since the previous Monday.

      B. The number of cardio and resistance training sessions your client completed since last Monday.

      C. The current nutrition targets of each of your clients (specified by you in your client's Target section in trainerize)

  4. Use this report to illustrate to your clients how close they were to their nutrition goals over the past week!
